Description

This is a sketch of an experimental camera I made!

Design Process

I wanted to make a camera that reflected on how cameras were used during the pandemic. We were all relying on our cameras and zoom to stay in touch with each other. I used the changing filters and multiple camera frames to represent a passage of time since we were all in quarantine for so long. Link to sketch: https://editor.p5js.org/kshin100/full/qM7ZEm44X

Description

This is a sketch of a data portrait I made!

Design Process

Over the course of a week I measured my step count. I used paw prints as a symbol of steps. The more high/low the paw print moves, the more steps I took that day. The color of the pawprints also indicate how many steps I took. The darker the pawprint, the less steps I took.

Reflections

In this exercise I learned how important it is to have an organized setup so I don't lose track of my assets.

This is a sketch of a game I made!

Design Process

Pigs have always been my favorite animals and I was inspired by the expression "when pigs fly". So I made a game where a pig is on a farm and is trying to catch a pair of wings so he can fly in the sky and get back to his owner.

Reflections

In this exercise I learned how important it is to create classes to organize my assets.
